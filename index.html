My Work



Kiwi Case Study

Kiwi, a health and wellbeing app with a focus on proper nutrition and meal planning. To better understand the problem, I conducted user research by posting a google survey for a wider range of users and interviewed three participants in the target market. With that information, I was able to create two user personas and identify the key features of the app.

Tools: Adobe XD; Photoshop CC; Optimal Card Sort; Google Survey

Lingo Case Study

Lingo, a children’s vocabulary app that provides a fun, educational way of learning everyday words and required site words for school. I was able to interview and conduct usability testing with two children in my target market and their parents. I felt the parents play a huge roll because they provided feedback on the children’s needs and behaviors that play a key role in comprehension.

Tools: Photoshop CC; Marvel
